Life in the UK online book

Make sure you are ready for the Life in the UK Test by reading the official handbook or the free Online Book.

The book covers a wide range of topics that will help you get ready for your British citizenship or permanent residency.

  • The process on becoming a citizen or permanent resident
  • The values and principles of the UK
  • Traditions and culture from around the UK
  • The events and people that have shaped the UK's history
  • The government and the law
  • Getting involved in your community

Life in the UK test

All questions in the mock exams are based on the official 3rd edition handbook ‘Life in the United Kingdom: A guide for new residents’.

There are 15 sample practice tests containing 24 questions each with a 45 minute countdown timer, just like in the real official test.

Each question is provided with an explanation to help memorize and understand the correct answers.

The Life in the UK book should be read prior to completing the practice tests.
